Day 6
To know the Love of Christ
6
Father, I ask that according to the riches of Your glory, You would grant the body of Muddy Creek Baptist church to be strengthened with might through Your Spirit in the inner man, that Christ may dwell in our hearts through faith, and that we, being rooted and grounded in love, may be able to comprehend with all the saints, what is the width and length and depth and height--
to know the Love of Christ,
which passes knowledge, that we may be filled with all the fullness of GOD.
Now to You, O' GOD, who is able to do exceedingly abundantly above all that we ask or think, according to the power that works in us, to You be glory in the Church by Christ Jesus throughout all ages, world without end. Amen. (Ephesians 3:14-21)
