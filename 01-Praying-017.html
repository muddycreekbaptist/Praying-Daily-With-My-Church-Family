Day 17
The unborn and all victims of abortion
17
God of Life, of spiritual life and physical life, we intercede for the safety of every child in the womb. This day we cry out for the unborn boys and girls. Each of these children are fearfully and wonderfully made for Your glory. Wrap Your protective arms around them. Please wake this nation up to stop murdering our children.
We cry out for the mothers and fathers who have been deceived by the demonic spirit of death into this sin and re crushed. Father, You see the millions of women who are living under the bondage of shame and guilt from their sins. Open their hearts to know that forgiveness is found in Jesus. Lift them out of the prison of despair. Bring healing to their souls and to their bodies. And Lord, forgive them, we ask. We know what it is like to be deceived and to walk in darkness. You forgave us and healed us. We ask for mercy and new life in them.

No heart is beyond Your saving arm. And turn the leaders of this nation to see the sanctity and beauty of every life, and give them the courage and wisdom to stand and protect the unborn. Amen. (Psalm 139:14-18; Exodus 21:22-25; 23:7; Proverbs 6:16-17; 24:11-12; Jeremiah 22:17)
